# Story 4.1: Cooking Log -- "Lo cocine!" & Rating

## Intent
**Epic Handle:** "Cook it, log it, grow from it"
**Story Handle:** "This story stamps your cooking passport and restocks the pantry shelves."

## Status: drafted

## User Story
As a user, I want to log a cooked meal with a rating and have my pantry deducted automatically, so that my data stays accurate and I can track my progress.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-1: Cooked It Button
- **Given** a recipe detail view
- **When** I tap "Lo cocine!"
- **Then** a confirmation modal appears asking for a rating (1-5 stars)

### AC-2: Meal Logged
- **Given** I submit a rating
- **When** the meal is logged
- **Then** a `users/{uid}/cookedMeals/{mealId}` document is created with: recipeName, recipeId, date, complexity, rating, cuisine, techniques, ingredientCanonicalIds

### AC-3: Pantry Deduction
- **Given** a logged meal
- **When** saved
- **Then** recipe ingredients are deducted from my pantry based on the recipe's ingredient quantities

### AC-4: Ingredient Removal on Zero
- **Given** a pantry deduction
- **When** an ingredient quantity reaches 0 or below
- **Then** the pantry item is removed

### AC-5: Success Confirmation
- **Given** a successful log
- **When** confirmed
- **Then** a success message is shown: "Registrado! Tu despensa se actualizo."

## Architectural ACs

- **AC-ARCH-LOC-1:** Cooking log service at `src/services/cooking-log.ts`
- **AC-ARCH-LOC-2:** Cooking log types at `src/types/cooking-log.ts`
- **AC-ARCH-LOC-3:** CookingLogStore at `src/stores/cookingLogStore.ts`
- **AC-ARCH-PATTERN-1:** Pantry deduction uses Firestore `runTransaction` (read current quantities, deduct, commit atomically)
- **AC-ARCH-PATTERN-2:** cookingLogStore action triggers pantryStore refresh after deduction
- **AC-ARCH-NO-1:** No separate "deduct pantry" button -- deduction is automatic on meal log

## Dev Notes

- The pantry deduction needs careful handling. Recipe ingredients have quantities, but pantry items also have quantities. The deduction subtracts recipe amounts from pantry amounts. If the pantry has 500g of chicken and the recipe uses 300g, the pantry should show 200g after.
- Use Firestore transactions for the deduction to prevent race conditions (user taps "Lo cocine!" twice quickly).
- The `ingredientCanonicalIds` in the cooked meal record is used by Story 4.2 for exploration profile updates.
- The rating is simple 1-5 stars. No text review for MVP.
- Consider disabling the "Lo cocine!" button after successful log to prevent double-logging.
